Overview

Purpose

Have you even wanted a place where you could look at poetry and maybe even write a little of your own? With the website I have in mind you will be able to browse through a couple sections of different posts of Poetry. It will also have a place where you can even practice writing your own poetry. It gives people the chance to explore in themselves the creativity they hold.

Audience

Our audience are most likely college students or a older demographic of people. They are looking for a place to read and create poetry. They want easy access to a place where they can accomidate this desire. They may need layouts and examples to help them get started creating their own poetry. Lack of experience in writing peotry would be the main concern for most people including lack of experience.

Content

Home page

The reason for people to come my site is to gain a firsthand experience of the wonderful world of poetry. There they will be able to read some different examples of poetry and learn of different poets. This will be an area where people can come to be creative and find their passion in writing poetry. The website will be designed so the first page introduces the audience to the website and gives a layout of what the people can find and expect from the website itself. It will have links to the other pages.

Images for the Home page

Poems and Poets

The second page will introduce the audience to different examples of poems and poets. Here is where they will get familiar with forms and styles of poetry that has been written over the years. Different poets and their stories such as Ralph Waldo Emmerson or Edgar Allen Poe. This part of the website will be changing over a period. To allow different Poets and poems to circulate.

Images for the Page 2

Creations

The third page will be focused on providing a space where the people will have a chance to write their own poetry. I am thinking of having a way for the people to be able to log into an account for a way to be able to save poems written.
